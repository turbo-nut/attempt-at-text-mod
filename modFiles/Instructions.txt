To install: 
1. Go to C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Risk of Rain 2\Risk of Rain 2_Data\StreamingAssets\Language OR 
	a.) Go to Properties under Risk Of Rain 2 on steam.
	b.) Go to browse local files.
	2. Go through Risk of Rain 2_Data.
	3. Go to StreamingAssets
	4. Go to Language.
4. Back up the original folder
5. Put the new "en" folder in.
6. Then you're done.
Make sure your game language is in english of course
